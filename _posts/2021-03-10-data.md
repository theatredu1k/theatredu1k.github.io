Le Journal Des "Peut-Êtres":<br> des "pas-tout-à-fait-mais-presque", pas fini, pas commencé, des "demain", des "hier", des "on-verra-bien"...<br>

This is a DIARY of des "Peut-Êtres": <br>, the ones who will "there-maybe" , the ones who are "not-even-there-yet", the ones who have "not-even-started", the ones who I have "just dreamed-of"...<br>

Un "Peut-Être" , perd son "Peut" pour devenir un "Être"
One who was a "May-Be" looses "May" and becomes a "Be-ing"
