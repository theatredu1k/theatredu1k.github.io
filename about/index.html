What do the objects think about us? :

I explore the complexity of the world through Puppetry and tell stories with everyday objects, new materials, data, sensors and actuators

This is a portfolio of investigations, props and becoming objects Traces of my research at the Royal College of Art about new Textile Materials from a puppetry perspective Testimonies about my exploration of programming language and electronic artefacts
