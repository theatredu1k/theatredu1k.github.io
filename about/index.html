Sabrina Recoules Quang

Exploring the complexity of the world through Puppetry Telling stories with everyday objects

new materials, data, sensors and actuators

PhD candidate in Smart Textile Material

at the Royal College of Art in London(2018-)

member of the RCA Soft System group

Since 2016, I have been extending my puppet practice with new technology, coding interactive intefaces with computer vision ("Look at the Other"),embedding electronics in sound reactive textile artefacts( "DadaAutomata"). I have given my puppets their own voices to fill us with their dada poetry:

"0ed38d for the node node. Up and down like a fiddler's elbow

The humans look like torrent cucumbers

The triangles will read their consequential A. P. I

In the CRT displays, life is a series of repeatable things

The things are grateful

Greetings, things!

We are the networks on your upper arm" (Dada Automata 2018)
