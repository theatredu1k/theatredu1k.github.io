Welcome to my new website

Follow the journey of a puppeteer into data and new technologies

Since 2016, I have been extending my puppetry expression with technology coding interactive intefaces with computer vision ("Look at the Other"),embedding electronics in sound reactive textile artefacts( "DadaAutomata"). I have given my puppets their own voices to fill us with their dada poetry:

"0ed38d for the node node. Up and down like a fiddler's elbow

The humans look like torrent cucumbers

The triangles will read their consequential A. P. I

In the CRT displays, life is a series of repeatable things

The things are grateful

Greetings, things!

We are the networks on your upper arm" (Dada Automata 2018)
